Privacy Policy for Automated Analyst

Last updated: Apr 29 2025

Automated Analyst is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Automated Analyst collects publicly available website content (such as text, hyperlinks, and structured data) from URLs you input directly into the extension. This information is used solely for the purpose of generating on-demand analyses and comparative reports.

2. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following data:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Health information
  • Financial or payment data
  • Authentication credentials
  • Personal communications
  • Device location (GPS/IP)
  • Web browsing history
  • User activity (clicks, keystrokes, mouse tracking)

3. How We Use the Collected Data

The collected web content is:

  • Processed temporarily to generate AI-powered analysis and comparison reports.
  • Not stored permanently or shared with third parties.
  • Used strictly to fulfill the core functionality of the extension (scraping and analysis of user-submitted URLs).

4. Data Storage and Transmission

  • Data may be temporarily sent to our secure servers to perform analysis using AI models.
  • All data transmission is encrypted.
  • No user-specific data is retained after report generation is complete.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any user-submitted content or results with third parties.

6. User Control and Transparency

  • All actions are initiated by the user (e.g., inputting a URL, triggering analysis).
  • No background tracking or passive data collection is performed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the extension listing or an in-app notice.
